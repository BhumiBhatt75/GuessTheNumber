4 digits · no leading zero · no repeated digits · no ascending consecutive (e.g. 7→8)

How to play
  1. Both players secretly choose a 4-digit number, then the game begins.
  2. Take turns guessing your opponent's number — you have 8 attempts each.
  3. Green — right digit, right position.
  4. Yellow — right digit, wrong position.
  5. Grey — digit not in the number at all.
  6. Use the digit tracker keypad to mark off eliminated digits.
  7. First to guess correctly wins! If both run out of attempts, it's a draw.
